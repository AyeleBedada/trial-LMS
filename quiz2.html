Architecture: Advanced Facts

Modern architecture embraces minimalism, functionality, and the use of innovative materials like glass, steel, and composites.

It integrates sustainability by focusing on energy efficiency, eco-friendly design, and adapting to climate needs.

Iconic structures like the Sydney Opera House or Burj Khalifa demonstrate the global influence and ambition of contemporary architecture.
