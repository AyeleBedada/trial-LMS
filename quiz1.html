Architecture: General Facts

Architecture is both an art and a science, shaping the built environment we live in. From houses to skyscrapers, it defines how we experience space.

It reflects cultural identity, technological progress, and human creativity. Every building tells a story of its time and its people.

Architects design spaces that balance functionality, safety, and aesthetics, creating structures that serve society in multiple ways.
